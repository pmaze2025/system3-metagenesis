# Templates

These are operational surfaces for SYSTEM3.

---

## session_start.txt

Declares a SYSTEM3 session.

---

## authority_declaration.txt

Defines user authority and limits.

---

## constraint_bundle.txt

Structures input into GO / LO / BO / SO.

---

## exit_system3.txt

Terminates SYSTEM3 jurisdiction.

---

## reenter_system3.txt

Starts a new session via BOOT.

---

## Usage

Templates are:

- minimal  
- declarative  
- non-instructional  

They do not add meaning.

They expose structure only.
